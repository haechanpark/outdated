P1.1.6 Project Compound

Early September 2018- Mid September 2018

Principles of Engineering
For the P1.1.6 project on compound machines, my group and I had the objective of creating a compound machine that can fulfill an every day function for a secret group known as the Members of the Compound. As a group member, I had the responsibility to both help construct the machine as well as monitor the progression of the online documentation. As a student, I learned how to create my first proper design brief for the POE curriculum. I also learned how to use VEX parts to create a machine for the first time. The project exemplified many skills as well, including cooperation, teamwork, diligency, and accuracy. The rubric for the project is located here.

P2.1.4 Aerodynamic Design

Late January 2019- Mid February 2019

Principles of Engineering
For project P2.1.4 project Aerodynamic Design, the group had the task of designing small rockets to fit the problem statement of designing a toy launchable rocket. As a group member, I designed my own rocket models for testing and also contributed to my portion of the online documentation, which included data collection and descriptions. In terms of learning, I learned how to design an aerodynamic object utilizing the laws learned in that unit and also learned how to use the aerodynamic formulas based around displacement and velocity. This project showed many skills as well, including teamwork, time management, and research abilities.
